Le Male by Jean Paul Gaultier is a Oriental Fougere fragrance.

Le Male was launched in 1995. The nose behind this fragrance is Francis Kurkdjian.

Middle notes are: Cinnamon, Orange, Blossom and Caraway.

Base notes are: Vanilla, Tonka Bean, Amber, Sandalwood and Cedar.
