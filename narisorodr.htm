Narciso Rodriguez For Her by Narciso Rodriguez is a Floral Woody Musk fragrance.

Narciso Rodriguez For Her was launched in 2003.

Narciso Rodriguez was created by Christine Nagel and Francis Kurkdjian.

Top notes are: African Orange flower, Osmanthus and Bergamot.

Middle notes are: Musk and Amber.

Base notes are: Vetiver, Vanille and Patchouli.

This perfume is the winner of 2 awards: FIFI Award Fragrance of The year.
